Recruitment: the most simplistically-complex game of the corporate world!

Recruitment: das einfach komplexeste Spiel der Geschäftswelt!

When we think of “hiring” a talent, we have a pre-defined skill checklist in mind for the talent to come in. But data says, most of the times, the hiring/line managers end up compromising on some/most of the skills required for a job role. Reasons can be many – lack of time, availability of skilled talent, budget or the RIGHT check!

Wenn wir daran denken, ein Talent einzustellen, haben wir eine Checkliste für die nötigen Fähigkeiten im Kopf, damit das Talent direkt einsteigen kann. Studen zeigen aber, dass die Personalverantwortlichen und Führungskräfte am Ende bei vielen Qualifikationen Kompromisse eingehen. Die Gründe können vielfältig sein – Zeitmangel, Verfügbarkeit von passenden Talenten, Budget oder der RICHTIGE Einstellungscheck!

We strongly believe that every hire counts and one wrong hire can spoil the game! We at Primed Talent aim at addressing the challenges of recruitment at all levels – entry to senior, and ensure new recruits create value for your business from their first day on the job!

Wir sind fest davon überzeugt, dass jedes Talent zählt und eine falsche Einstellung das Spiel verderben kann! Wir bei Primed Talent zielen darauf ab, die Herausforderungen des Recrutings auf allen Ebenen anzugehen – auf allen Ebenen. Wir stellen sicher, dass neue Mitarbeiter vom ersten Tag an einen Mehrwert für Ihr Unternehmen schaffen!

Source, Train, Onboard (STO)

  • We mutually agree with our Client on the “acceptable gap” in the current skill level of the candidate that can be bridged via training
  • We agree with our Client on the curriculum and the duration of training
  • We source candidates as per the agreed criteria, who undergo a selection process to check on their current skill levels
  • The ones that clear the selection process are issued an “Expression of Interest (EOI)” by our Client. The EOI implies that if a candidate completes the training successfully, then she/he will be hired by the Client as employees on their payroll at the role and compensation mentioned in the EOI. This entire process can be run virtually
    • For freshers, a stipend can be paid during the training cycle. This is discretionary
    • For experienced professionals, they will be hired on contract by us and paid a salary during training that is normally the same as their current salary. Salary post training, when they join on the Client payroll, is negotiated during the sourcing cycle itself and is within a range provided by the Client
  • We train these selected candidates as per the mutually agreed training plan with our Client. Training can be done virtually or in class rooms or in the blended mode
  • Post successful completion of training, these candidates get hired by our Client and get deployed on projects or undergo further training (specific to project)
  • We are compensated by our Client for our Sourcing and Training efforts and also for the payroll charges during the training period
